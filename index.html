About the Course

The Generative AI for Work & Research course is an 8-week live cohort program offered by GRAPH Courses, University of Geneva. It focuses on leveraging no-code AI tools for task automation, research, and communication. The course provides training in:

  • Understanding large language models (LLMs) and no-code AI tools
  • Automated data analysis and visualization
  • Research text analysis and knowledge synthesis
  • Custom GPTs and chatbot building
  • AI-powered task automation
  • AI for scientific communication and outreach
  • A capstone project and peer showcase

The program emphasizes hands-on experience, peer collaboration, and real-world applications of AI for research productivity.
